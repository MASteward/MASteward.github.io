About Me

I served five years in the United States Army in the prestigious 82nd Airborne Division stationed at Fort Bragg North Carolina. During my service I deployed twice to Iraq in support of Operation Iraqi Freedom.

After my service, I returned home to enroll in the local community college before transferring to the University of Texas at Austin. In 2015, I graduated from with a Bachelors of Arts in Political Science and Sociology.
